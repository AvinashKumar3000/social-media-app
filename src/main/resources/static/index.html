How Pulse works

Three ideas. No infinite scroll.

Every part of Pulse is designed to end, not trap you — a feed that finishes, spaces that stay small, and moments that don't outlive their relevance.

◐

Rooms

Small, invite-only spaces capped at 20 people. No public profiles to perform for — just the people already in your life.

◑

Echoes

Photos and updates that quietly disappear after 48 hours. Share the ordinary moment without it becoming a permanent record.

◒

Signal

A plain chronological feed. Nothing is boosted, nothing is buried — you see everything, in the order it happened.
